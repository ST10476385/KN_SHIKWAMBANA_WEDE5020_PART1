About Baked by Ruth

A tribute to our beloved Ruth - a woman whose passion for baking created connections, brought joy, and built a legacy that lives on in every loaf.

Ruth's Story

Born and raised in the heart of Limpopo, Ruth developed an ambitious spirit and an unwavering passion for baking that would touch countless lives. Every morning before dawn, she would rise to prepare her famous fat cakes and scones for local factories and schools.

On days when school was out, I would join her in the kitchen at sunrise, watching in wonder as she measured ingredients with the precision of love, kneaded dough with patient hands, and greeted each customer like family. Ruth had a gift - she could turn simple ingredients into moments of joy.

Baking wasn't just Ruth's profession; it was her way of connecting with people, uplifting spirits, and sharing her dreams. She always envisioned building something lasting, turning her love for baking into a legacy that would outlive her.

Though Ruth passed away in July 2025, her spirit lives on in every recipe, every warm greeting, and every moment of joy our baked goods bring to families. Baked by Ruth is more than a bakery - it's the fulfillment of her dream.

Our Mission

To honor Ruth's legacy by creating exceptional baked goods that bring families together, support our community, and spread the warmth and love that Ruth embodied in every recipe she shared.

Our Vision

To become the heart of our community's celebrations and daily joys, preserving traditional South African baking while creating new memories for future generations to treasure.

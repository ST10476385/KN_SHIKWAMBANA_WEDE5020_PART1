Getting to Our Main Bakery

From N1 Highway: Take R101 exit toward Polokwane. Continue on R101 for 8km, turn left at Heritage Street.

Public Transport: Take BRT Bus Route 12 to Heritage Street stop, walk 200m north.

Landmarks: Located next to Heritage Primary School, opposite Pick n Pay Heritage Plaza.

Delivery Service

We deliver within 15km of our main bakery. Minimum order R150. Delivery fee R50. Free delivery for orders over R500.
